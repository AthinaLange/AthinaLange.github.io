  1. Lange, A.M.Z., Fiedler, J.W., Merrifield, M.A., and Guza, R.T. (2023)

    UAV-video based estimates of nearshore bathymetry

    Coastal Engineering

    • Wave crests can be obtained from timestacks with the help of a U-Net neural network.
    • Surfzone bathymetry can be well estimated remotely with UAVs ( ~ 0.24m).
    • Crest-tracking surfzone and offshore cBathy estimates give nearshore bathymetries.

  2. Lange, A.M.Z., Fiedler, J.W., Merrifield, M.A., and Guza, R.T. (2022)

    Estimating runup with limited bathymetry

    Coastal Engineering

    • 24 composite bathymetries are evaluated with the SWASH 1D model.
    • Setup and infragravity swash are dependent on offshore bathymetry.
    • A frequency-weighted energy spectra parameterization of the 2% exceedance level of runup performs significantly better than a bulk parameterization.
    • Including both foreshore beach slope and surfzone slope improves the skill of runup parameterizations.
